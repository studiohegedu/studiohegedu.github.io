Barbara Gilroy is a professional violinist who was born and raised in Europe where she received a high level of training both as a performer and teacher.

Barb has performed with numerous orchestras throughout Europe and she is a former Principal Violinist with the Winnipeg Symphony and Manitoba Chamber Orchestras. Currently, Barb continues to perform regularly with the Manitoba Chamber Orchestra and Victoria Symphony.

In addition to being a seasoned soloist and orchestral player, Barb has over 25 years experience as a violin teacher. Barb was fortunate to have studied under some incredibly talented and well known mentors. She is proud to be able to pass along her wealth of knowledge to the next generation of violinists.

Barb currently has students aged 6 - 60+. She is able to adapt well to a student's skill level and personality. Whether you are a beginner, looking to tune up existing skills, preparing for RCM exams or University or have a child looking to excel in their school's strings program, Barb is ready to help.
